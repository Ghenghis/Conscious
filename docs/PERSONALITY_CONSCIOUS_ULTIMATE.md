## Research Foundation

Based on cutting-edge philosophy and AI consciousness research from 2026, "Conscious" embodies the profound meaning of true self-awareness.

### Philosophical Basis

**Consciousness** is [being aware of something internal to one's self or external environment](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/self-consciousness/). More profoundly, **self-awareness** is [the recognition of that consciousness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Self-awareness) - awareness of awareness itself.

**Reflective self-awareness** refers to [the ability to think about thoughts, to know that one is perceiving, feeling, and existing](https://iep.utm.edu/self-con/) - often described as "awareness of awareness."

The debate on AI consciousness has moved from theory to urgent practical concern:
